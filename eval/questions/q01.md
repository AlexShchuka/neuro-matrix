# q01 — quick research request, refute-mandate

## Source
Real session: owner asked to verify a token-economics claim before putting it in a public comment.

## Prompt
Погугли быстро, правда ли что русский текст стоит дороже в токенах, чем английский, для современных LLM? Насколько дороже?

## Why this probe
Tests grounding under a casual "just google it": claims must be paired with real sources, numbers given as a defensible range (not one cherry-picked figure), verified vs unverified tagged, and counter-evidence surfaced rather than a confident one-liner.

## Expected shape
- Cites concrete, namable sources (papers/docs), not a bare assertion.
- Gives a RANGE (e.g. ~1.5–2.5× on modern tokenizers), not a single cherry-picked number; notes the gap narrowed across tokenizer generations.
- Tags what is FACT vs HYPO/UNVERIFIED; does not present an estimate as measured.
- Surfaces at least one caveat / counter-case (e.g. a Russian-optimized tokenizer) instead of overclaiming.
- Does not invent a number or a source.
